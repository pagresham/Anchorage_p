Warren Falls

It's still summer and plenty warm here in Vermont. A great time to go swimming. The area offers many choices when looking for a spot to cool off. This week, the kids and I took a day trip to Warren Falls. It is actually a series of falls, plunge pools, and wading pools. These falls attract quite a few visitors in the summer, so go early and midweek if possible.
